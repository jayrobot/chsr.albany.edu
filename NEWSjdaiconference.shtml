CHSR was represented by Susan Ehrhard-Dietzel, Ph.D. at the 2015 Juvenile Detention Alternatives Initiative Inter-Site Conference in Arizona.

In late September the Juvenile Detention Alternatives Initiative (JDAI) Conference was held in Phoenix, Arizona. The conference serves to both connect practitioners in the detention reform movement and to strengthen their strategies and networks. Among the topics discussed were “Developing, Implementing, and Using Risk Assessments and Disposition Matrices” and “When Helping Hurts: The Dangers of Needs-Based Confinement”.

This conference is very timely as the President of the United States has recently proclaimed October 2015 Youth Justice Awareness Month.

"NOW, THEREFORE, I, BARACK OBAMA, President of the United States of America, by virtue of the authority vested in me by the Constitution and the laws of the United States, do hereby proclaim October 2015 as National Youth Justice Awareness Month. Dr. Ehrhard-Dietzel, is the principle investigator for CHSR on the Albany County Juvenile Detention Alternatives Initiative (JDAI).
