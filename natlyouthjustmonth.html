October is National Youth Justice Month!

In honor of this month-long observance, CHSR is highlighting the wonderful work of our staff who are dedicated towards improving outcomes for youth. Kenneth Robin and Chris Rees collaborate extensively with the Albany City School District and local nonprofit organizations to enhance educational opportunities for youth. Susan Ehrhard-Dietzel is the co-coordinator of the Juvenile Detention Alternatives Initiative (JDAI) in Albany County, which works to reduce the number of youth in detention and to address issues of racial and ethnic disparity in the juvenile justice system. She recently presented at a statewide conference on youth justice and is traveling to New Mexico this month as part of her work with the JDAI. These individuals are among those on our staff who participate in local and regional workgroups in an effort to improve outcomes for youth.
