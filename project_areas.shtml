Project Areas  

Education & Early Childhood Development

CHSR’s expertise in evaluations of education and early childhood development include both process and outcome studies. We work closely with the Albany City School District to inform and assess the implementation of new initiatives. We also partner with local and state organizations to evaluate training and professional development programs.

Active Projects

  • New York State Birth Through Five Preschool Development Grant

Completed Projects

  • 21st Century Community Learning Centers
  • Albany School Improvement Grant Program Evaluation
  • My Brother’s Keeper Challenge Grant (MBKCG)
  • The City of Albany School District Data Analysis

Children, Family, & Elder Services

CHSR’s work in the area of children and familiy services includes evaluation and research, and systems and software development for two statewide Healthy Families America programs. Additional projects in this area reflect our expertise in evaluations of programs designed to strengthen families and enhance interagency collaboration in the provision of services.

Active Projects

  • E-MDT Initiative Preliminary Evaluation
  • Healthy Families New Jersey Evaluation
  • Healthy Families New York Evaluation
  • Information System for Prevention Programs
  • Kinship Navigator
  • The National Youth in Transition Database (NYTD)

Completed Projects

  • Evaluation of New York State Family Resource Centers
  • Evaluation of CPS/DV Collocation
  • Adolescent Pregnancy & Prevention Services
  • Albany County Human Services Department Evaluation

Health Care Services

CHSR’s work in the area of health care evaluations of programs designed to assist and enhance the development of healthy lifestyles. Our portfolio includes assessments of eating habits and access to nutritious foods, and access to health care services, including mental health care.

Active Projects

  • Delivery System Reform Incentive Payment Program (DSRIP)
  • Greater Rochester Opioid Prevention

Completed Projects

  • Assessment of Health Services for Veterans in Rural Upstate New York
  • Center of Excellence for Maternal and Infant Health
  • ECHO Collaborative
  • OPWDD Direct Care Employee Trust Survey

Economic & Community Development

CHSR’s scope of work in Economic and Community Development covers a diverse range of topics that have shaped successful partnerships with organizations such as the NYS Department of Health and the Corporation for National and Community Service in an effort to identify strengths, challenges and best practices. We collect, research and evaluate data using multiple methods that include qualitative, process and outcome studies.

Active Projects

  • Capital Area Poverty Reduction Initiative (CAPRI)
  • MRT Supportive Housing Initiative
  • Evaluation of the Wage Center

Completed Projects

  •  Evaluation of the Wage Center
  • Albany County Equity Agenda
  • Evaluation of the SEED Program
  • Qualitative Research on Housing Needs of Grandfamilies

Criminal Justice, Juvenile Justice, & Youth Development

CHSR’s portfolio includes process and outcome studies of local and state initiatives designed to address the impact of juvenile justice programs on youth. We conduct evaluations of prevention programs as well as models of organizational and systems change.

Active Projects

  • Albany County Juvenile Detention Alternatives Initiative (JDAI)
  • Evaluation of the Summer Youth Employment Program
  • Training Needs of Victim Service Providers
  • Victim Legal Assistance Network Development

Completed Projects

  • Her Justice
  • Innovations in Victim Services for Underserved Populations
  • Rural Law Initiative Collaboration
  • Re-Entering the Albany Community through Treatment (REACT) Evaluation
