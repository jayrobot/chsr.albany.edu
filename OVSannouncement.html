CHSR is awarded Office of Victim Services Contract

The Center for Human Services Research has been awarded a contract to conduct a needs assessment on services for crime victims in New York State outside of NYC. CHSR is part of a team with the New York State Office of Victim Services (OVS), the Empire Justice Center (EJC), and Probono.net that has received grant funding from the U.S. Department of Justice, Office for Victims of Crime to create and implement a crime victims’ legal network. During the course of the 18-month project, CHSR will work to document services that are currently available, gauge the level of need based on types and locations of crime victims, and identify service and accessibility gaps. The needs assessment will guide the development and improvement of services, which will be implemented by OVS, EJC and Probono.net.

The research team for this project is comprised of Drs. Brad Watts and Susan Dietzel.
