Aviles joined CHSR in 2001 as one of the original interviewers for the Healthy Families New York Randomized Trial.  She studied early education and dance at the University of Buffalo and is working towards a degree in social work at Buffalo State College.  Currently, Maria is working on the Healthy Families Year 15 follow- up interviews.  Along with her professional endeavors, Maria also enjoys a busy family and personal life.  She is active in several civic and cultural organizations.
