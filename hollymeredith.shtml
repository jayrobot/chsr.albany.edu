Holly Meredith joined CHSR in 2019 as a researcher on the Preschool Development Grant Birth Through Five project. Before coming to CHSR, Holly worked at the Evaluation Consortium, the Child Research and Study Center, and at the University at Albany, SUNY on several projects involving technology, literacy, drugs and mental health, Head Start, and children and adolescents with disabilities. Her work has been published in scholarly journals and academic books, and she has presented at international and regional conferences.

Holly received her Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from New York University and a Master’s degree in Educational Psychology and Methodology from the University at Albany, SUNY, where she is also currently a doctoral candidate.
