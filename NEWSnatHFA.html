CHSR's John Heck invited to present at the National Healthy Families America Leadership Conference in Chicago in November 2015.

Healthy Families America has invited John Heck to the National Leadership Conference in Chicago. John will present at a workshop at the conference entitled “An Information Tool that Works” that will highlight the nationally recognized HFNY MIS that has been developed in New York over the past 20 years and continues to set “ best practice” for data collection methods and techniques in home visiting. This presentation will again place NY on the national stage as a leader in the HFA network.
