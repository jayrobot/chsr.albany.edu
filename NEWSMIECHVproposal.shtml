CHSR to present at the 2019 Maternal, Infant, and Early Childhood Home Visiting (MIECHV) State, Territory, and Tribal All Grantee Meeting

CHSR Senior Research Scientist Margaret Gullick, Ph.D and Healthy Families New York (HFNY) Program Managers Annemarie Correa (Erie County) and Heather Daly (Schenectady County) will lead a session at the 2019 Maternal, Infant,and Early Childhood Home Visiting (MIECHV) State, Territory, and Tribal All Grantee Meeting, on February 27, 2019 at the Baltimore Marriott Waterfront Hotel in Baltimore, MD. Their presentation is titled: “Key factors in home visitor retention: Perspectives from programs and research.”
