Steven O’Neill is the Central New York data collector for the NYS Systems of Care project. His primary role is to observe child and family team meetings and administer surveys to family and team members who are participating in the wraparound process. His background includes working as field research interviewer for NORC at the University of Chicago, conducting interviews for the National Survey of Early Care and Education. Steven has a BA in Public Policy from SUNY Empire State College.
