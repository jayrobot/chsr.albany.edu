Family Assessment Response Evaluation

The CHSR in collaboration with the OCFS conducted an evaluation of a differential response program for child protection services (CPS) called Family Assessment Response (FAR).

While a number of counties will be using FAR, the study includes the first five counties to incorporate FAR services into their CPS programs. OCFS is conducting an evaluation that will include a process study of the implementation of the FAR Demonstration and an impact study that analyzes its effects on families.  A control group design has been established in one of the counties. 

Evaluation & Research
CHSR involvement includes advising on the client satisfaction survey design, developing study protocols and developing relevant materials, then administering the survey and managing the data. Multiple data collection and outreach techniques were used in an effort to take advantage of available technology and client preferences. The techniques included the use of home telephones, cell phones, text messaging, email contact and an online survey, as well as mail correspondence and a mail-in survey. 

Systems & Software Development
CHSR designed a customized database for tracking client contact information and interactions, as well as for creating tables for regular reporting on the data collection process. We designed and maintained four versions of an online survey, control and experimental group in both English and Spanish. The data was imported, managed and submitted to OCFS on a monthly basis.
