Healthy Family's MIS


MIS

The Center for Human Services Research (CHSR) has developed a Web-based MIS application that can be used "as is" or can be customized to your specific needs.

Details

Our Healthy Family's application is extremely versatile, easy to use, and powerful. It's smart enough to calculate gestational age if a child wasn't born full-term, incorporates a simple case home page allowing easy access to everything concerning a case in just a click or two, and has reports for nearly every need, including MIECV.

Our Clients

CHSR has developed and continues to manage/maintain the Healthy Family web applications for both New Jersey and New York.
