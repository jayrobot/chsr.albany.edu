Corinne joined CHSR in 2005 and worked on many projects and studies on a variety of research topics that include child maltreatment prevention, child mental health, and nutrition. She managed the HFNY RCT data collection for Y15 and is currently providing training and support to HFNY staff on the use of the Managing Information System (MIS) application. Corinne participates in HFNY Central Administration, an interdisciplinary team of HFNY program administrators, trainers, and evaluators that sets policy and program practices.

Corinne received her Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Psychology from the Universidad Argentina J. F. Kennedy- Buenos Aires, Argentina.
