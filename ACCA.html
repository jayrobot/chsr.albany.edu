Re-Entering the Albany Community through Treatment (REACT) Evaluation

CHSR is working with the Addictions Care Center of Albany (ACCA) and other community providers to evaluate REACT (Re-Entering the Albany Community through Treatment).

REACT, which is supported by the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA), provides education, case management, and integrated treatment for the substance abusing offender population reentering the community.

Evaluation & Research

The evaluation is designed to provide useful information to inform project implementation and measure project outcomes. Specifically, CHSR analyzes and reports on data collected from structured interviews and client focus groups. The evaluation provides data on project implementation; achievement of client and program goals and objectives; adherence to treatment model fidelity; the level of client satisfaction; and assessment of cultural competence, quality assurance and regulatory controls.
