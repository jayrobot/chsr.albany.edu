2018 Statewide Annual Report by the Independent Evaluator of the New York State Delivery System Reform Incentive Program (DSRIP)

CHSR is conducting the implementation and process component of the independent evaluation of the DSRIP program, which has the goal of restructuring the Medicaid health care delivery system. The evaluation will continue through 2021.
