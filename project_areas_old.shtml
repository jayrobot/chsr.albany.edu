Economic & Community Development

CHSR’s scope of work in Economic and Community Development covers a diverse range of topics that have shaped successful partnerships with organizations such as the NYS Department of Health and the Corporation for National and Community Service in an effort to identify strengths, challenges and best practices. We collect, research and evaluate data using multiple methods that include qualitative, process and outcome studies.

Completed Projects

  • Evaluation of the SEED Program
