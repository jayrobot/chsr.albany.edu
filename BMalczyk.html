Ben works with youth from the Liberty Partnership Program on a participatory action research project assessing community perceptions of Albany High School. The project seeks to add community voice and perspective to the Albany Promise Evaluation. Ben has also been involved with the Data Committee for the Albany Promise Evaluation. Ben received his Bachelor’s degree in Marriage, Family, and Human Development from Brigham Young University, and his Master’s in Social Work from the University at Albany SUNY. He is a Ph.D. student in the School of Social Welfare at the University at Albany SUNY.
