Families Together Annual Conference in New York State (2019).

Ruth Lindenfelser, CHSR Senior Research Support Specialist, presented at the Families Together in New York State 2019 Annual Conference on Monday, May 6, 2019. Ruth co-presented a session called “Why Youth Peer Advocates?” In her portion of the presentation, Ruth focused on the work of youth peer advocates in the New York State Systems of Care High Fidelity Wraparound project.
