Julia assists with data collection for the National Youth in Transition Database. Her previous graduate assistantship was with the Liberty Partnerships Program, working as a data reporter. Julia has interned at Albany Medical Center cardiac ICU and is currently an intern at Montessori Magnet School.

Julia received her Bachelor’s degree in sociology with a minor in psychology from the University at Albany in May 2014. This is her second year working towards an MSW with a clinical concentration in the School of Social Welfare at the University at Albany.
