Chris works on Albany Promise and other related projects as a data analyst. He received his Bachelor’s degree in Sociology at Western Washington University and his Master’s degree in Sociology at the University at Albany, SUNY, where he is currently a Ph.D. student. His research focuses on the spatial and network dynamics that influence crime and social control. His dissertation centers on the relationship between residential and workplace neighborhoods and their collective influence on processes related to social disorganization.
