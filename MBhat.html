Meera is currently working on an evaluation of the SEED (Small Enterprise Economic Development) Program. SEED is a “character-based” lending program for supporting and financing entrepreneurs who are outside the purview of traditional sources of credit. Meera received her Master's degree in Social Entrepreneurship and her Bachelor's degree in Social Work with a specialization in Rural Development at the Tata Institute of Social Sciences in Mumbai. She is a Ph.D. student in the School of Social Welfare at the University at Albany, SUNY.
