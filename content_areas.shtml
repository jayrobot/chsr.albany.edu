Content Areas

CHSR has expertise and conducts research in the following content areas:

Behavioral Health Services

CHSR works with agencies and organizations across the state on investigative studies designed to inform and educate communities and evaluate program implementation to improve behavioral health for adults, adolescents, and children. For example, CHSR has evaluated multiple SAMHSA System of Care expansion grants throughout NYS, conducting both implementation, fidelity, and outcome evaluations. Services provided have included conducting services observations, documentation review, stakeholder interviews, statewide and local surveys, and the development of case and program management software. SAMHSA System of Care Expansion Grants

 

Children, Family, & Elder Services

CHSR’s work in the area of children and family services includes ongoing evaluation and research and systems and software development for Healthy Families New York, a statewide child abuse and neglect prevention program. Additional projects in this area reflect our expertise in evaluations of programs designed to strengthen families, prevent and respond to elder abuse, and enhance interagency collaboration in the provision of services. Healthy Families New York Evaluation

 

Criminal & Juvenile Justice, Services

CHSR’s portfolio addresses the impact of initiatives designed to support victims of crime the providers who serve them. Our work includes needs assessments, training evaluations, and outcome studies. CHSR also conducts process and outcome studies of local and state initiatives designed to impact youth involvement in the juvenile and criminal justice systems. Victim Legal Assistance Network Development Albany County Juvenile Justice Coordination and Research

 

Equity-Focused Evaluation

CHSR works with groups focused on improving systems and structures to ensure equity in client access, service delivery, and outcomes. For example, CHSR has partnered with administrative and faculty groups conducting self-studies within UAlbany, and with external partners examining issues and topics across local, national, and international systems. Albany County Equity project HHMI Self-Study

 

Education & Early Childhood Development

CHSR’s expertise in evaluations of education and early childhood development includes both process and outcome studies. We work with school districts and the early childhood care and education system to inform and assess the implementation of new and existing initiatives. We also partner with local and state organizations to evaluate training and professional development programs. NYSB5 Preschool Development Grant

 

Health & Healthcare Services

CHSR’s work in the area of healthcare includes evaluations of programs designed to assist and enhance both access to healthcare services, including housing, mental health care, and overdose recovery services; and the development of healthy lifestyles, such as healthy eating habits and access to nutritious foods. DSRIP (Delivery System Reform Incentive Payment Program) MRT Supportive Housing Initiative
