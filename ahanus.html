Alexa joined CHSR as a graduate research assistant in September 2011. She is primarily involved in a project funded by OJJDP to evaluate the effects of a parent engagement initiative on mentoring outcomes.  She is also involved in the menu labeling evaluation project.   
Alexa is a doctoral student in the Counseling Psychology program at the University at Albany.  She received her MA in Counseling Psychology from Towson University in Baltimore, MD, and her BA in Psychology from Minnesota State University.  Her research interests include racial identity development and racism prevention, as well as clinical supervision and training.
