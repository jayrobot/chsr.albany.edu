Preventive Contracts Consistent With County Outcomes

CHSR collaborated with the University at Albany, SUNY’s Professional Development Program and the New York State Office of Children and Family Services to support local districts and agencies in child welfare preventive services.

As the evaluation arm of the project, CHSR developed and implemented a comprehensive evaluation of the services provided, including workshops, training sessions, and technical support on county planning, outcome-based services, logic models, and Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI).

Evaluation & Research

The CHSR evaluation included three of the four levels of Kirkpatrick’s training evaluation model: reaction, learning, and behavior. Data collection methods included participant reaction questionnaires, content analysis, pre and post test surveys, 30 day and 6 month follow-up online surveys, and document reviews.
