The National Youth in Transition Database

Deborah works on the National Youth in Transition Database project conducting interviews with youth who have experienced the foster care system. She collaborates with family members and caseworkers to locate eligible youth and encourage them to participate. She also provides other administrative support to the project as needed. Her history of working with children and youth of diverse backgrounds helps make her a relatable and engaging interviewer.

On her down time she enjoys nature walks, community volunteering, fishing, spending quality time with her family and dog.
