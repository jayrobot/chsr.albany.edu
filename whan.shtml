Woojae is working on the PATH evaluation, a SAMSHA funded project of the Addiction Care Center of Albany to serve the homeless population with substance abuse and mental health issues. Prior to joining CHSR, Woojae worked at Hopewell Center (a comprehensive community mental health agency) for three years as a case manager.  

Woojae received his Bachelor’s degree in Social Work from Soongsil University in South Korea and his MSW from Washington University in St. Louis. He is a Ph.D. student in the School of Social Welfare at the University at Albany, SUNY.
