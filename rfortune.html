Specialization: Short-term Treatment and Social Work Education

Anne E. Fortune teaches research and social work practice at the undergraduate and graduate levels. Her research is in the areas of task-centered practice, termination of social work treatment, aging, and field education. She is editor of Task-Centered Practice with Families and Groups (1985), and co-author (with William J. Reid) of Social Work Research (1998). She is past editor of The Journal of Social Work Education and current editor of Social Work Research. She completed her MSW and Ph.D. at the University of Chicago.
