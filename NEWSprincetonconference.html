CHSR's Senior Research Scientist, Dr. Sandra McGinnis has been invited to serve on a panel at a conference at Princeton.

Based on her work on the MRT/Supportive Housing Study, Dr. McGinnis has been invited to serve on a panel at a conference at Princeton on "The Economics of addressing Social Determinants of Health" in May, 2018.

The conference is an invitation only gathering of about 150 health economists and health service researchers.
