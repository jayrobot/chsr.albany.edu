Ellen joined CHSR in 2011 to lead the CPS/DV Collocation study. She has worked in a variety of research settings, including university and government evaluation departments both in the United States and Australia. Prior to coming to CHSR, Ellen served as an independent evaluation consultant in Minnesota. She has worked in areas including human services systems and coordination, health care access for the underserved, health improvement initiatives, and disability and mental health services. She has experience with a number of quantitative and qualitative data collection and analysis methods, as well as skills in program planning and policy development.
