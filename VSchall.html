Currently, Victoria is working on the Juvenile Justice Mobile Response Team. Before coming to CHSR, she was a staff member at Crossroads for Youth, a non-profit residential facility for youth adjudicated delinquent in Michigan. She has also worked on a school to prison pipeline intervention evaluation and was a discussion leader for Introduction to Criminal Law for the School of Criminal Justice. Victoria is a Ph.D. student in School of Criminal Justice at the University at Albany, SUNY. She received her Bachelor of Art degrees in English and Criminal Justice at Michigan State University, and her Master of Arts degree in Criminal Justice from University at Albany, SUNY.
