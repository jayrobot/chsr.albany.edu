Cori Anne Robohn

IT Tech Specialist

E-mail: crobohn@albany.edu
Telephone: 518-591-8610

 

Specialization: Tech Support, Data Collection, Focus Groups

 

Cori joined CHSR in 2012. She is currently the lead tech support specialist on several MIS applications and manages the HFNY website. Cori has been involved in a number of other CHSR projects, including NYTD, Community Nutrition Assessment and Innovations in Victim Services for Underserved Populations. She is experienced in website management and design, client/customer relations, focus group facilitating and data collection.

Cori obtained her education from Hudson Valley Community College in Liberal Arts and Business Administration. Also, she possesses a HTML Certification and Web design from CodeAcademy and W3 Schools.
