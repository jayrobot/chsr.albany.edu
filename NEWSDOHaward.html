The Center for Human Services Research (CHSR) was awarded a new grant by the NYS Department of Health

The Center for Human Services Research (CHSR) has been awarded a new grant by the NYS Department of Health to conduct an evaluation to assess the implementation, impact, effectiveness and cost savings of a MRT (Medicaid Redesign Team) Supportive Housing Initiative.
CHSR is teaming with the UA Institute for Health System Evaluation which specializes in health care cost analyses and CARES, Inc., an agency devoted to ending homelessness in 14 counties in NYS. The award is for $766,493 over 3 years.
