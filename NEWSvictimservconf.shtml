CHSR was represented by Susan Ehrhard-Dietzel, Ph.D. at the New York State Office of Victim Services 2017 Conference: Serving the Underserved: Increasing Access to Victim Services

The Victim Legal Assistance Network project is a collaborative effort between the New York State Office of Victim Services (OVS), the Empire Justice Center (EJC), Pro Bono Net (PBN) and CHSR researchers to develop a comprehensive, collaborative network to meet the civil legal needs of crime victims in New York State, outside of New York City. The grant partners recently presented their work at the New York State Office of Victim Services 2017 Conference: Serving the Underserved: Increasing Access to Victim Services.
