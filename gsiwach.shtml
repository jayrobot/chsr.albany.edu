Garima works on data collection and analysis on the Victim Legal Assistance Network Development project, and also provides assistance on several education and training area projects.

Garima received her Master's degree in Economics from the University of Mumbai, and Bachelor's degree with honors in Economics from the University of Delhi (India). She is currently a Ph.D. candidate of Economics at the University at Albany, SUNY, where her research focuses on studying labor market and recidivism impacts of criminal background checks for employment in New York State.
